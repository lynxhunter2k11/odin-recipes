Christmas Cake

Ingredients

  • mixed dried fruit 500g
  • flour 100g
  • brandy 100ml
  • 2 eggs
  • mixed christmas spices
  • sugar 200g
  • butter 250g

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 150°C/150°C Fan
  2. soak fruit overnight in brandy
  3. whisk together eggs, butter and sugar
  4. slowly whisk four and spices into the mixture
  5. turn fruit into mixture
  6. place mixture in cake tin and bake for 2 hours

P.S. Recipe may not lead to tasty cake. Christmas cake has a lot of steps and so shortcuts may have been taken.
