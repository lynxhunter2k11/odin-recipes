Chai Recipe

Ingredients

  • cinnamon 50g
  • cardamon 25g
  • black pepper 15g
  • sugar 75g
  • ginger 50g
  • cloves 15g
  • 3 standard tea bags
  • milk 500ml
  • water 500ml

Steps

  1. crush the ginger, black pepper and cardamon in a mortar and pestal
  2. heat water in a saucepan with all the spices and sugar
  3. simmer for 5 mins
  4. add milk and teabags
  5. simmer for 2 mins
  6. serve

P.S. Recipe may not lead to delicious Chai. Recipe is entirely for the purpose of preacticing html and not to be considered proper method. Drink entirely at your own risk.
