Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • cooked dry rice
  • oil
  • peas
  • bacon
  • soy sauce
  • egg

Steps

  1. chop bacon intol small cubes and fry on high heat
  2. once bacon begins to brown add rice and oil and fry until rice begins to brown
  3. add egg and fry to half cooked and then break apart using wooden spoon and mix throughout rice
  4. add soy sauce and peas and continually stir for 5 mins while cooking
  5. serve

P.S. this recipe should work although could do with some spices
